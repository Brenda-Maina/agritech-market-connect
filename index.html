Welcome to AgriTech Market Connect

This platform connects farmers directly with markets, enhancing their access to customers while improving efficiency.

Join us in our mission to transform agriculture with modern technology.

About the AgriTech Project

Status: The AgriTech Market Connect project is in an active iteration phase, focusing on user feedback and adaptation.

Key Achievements:

  • Conducted workshops for over 75 farmers on modern farming techniques.
  • Established partnerships with local grocery chains for produce purchasing.
  • Created a network of "Digital Champions" to assist local farmers.

Challenges and Mitigation:

  • Digital Literacy Barrier: Introduced Digital Champions for support.
  • Connectivity Issues: Piloted features for offline synchronization.
  • Building Trust in Digital Transactions: Implemented a hybrid transaction model.
