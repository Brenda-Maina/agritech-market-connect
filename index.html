Challenges & Mitigations

Navigating the Hard Problems

Every rollout surfaces real barriers. Here's how we're meeting each challenge with practical, community-grounded solutions.

📚

Digital Literacy Barrier

+

A significant portion of farmers, particularly those over 50, continue to struggle with navigating the app independently, hindering full utilization of features like dynamic pricing and inventory listing.

Our Mitigation Strategy

We are deploying our newly trained Digital Champions to lead smaller, weekly neighborhood clinics. We've also created a simplified, picture-based user guide in the local dialect to complement one-on-one support.

📶

Intermittent Connectivity Issues

+

While the app's offline mode is improved, synchronization errors can still occur in pockets with extremely unstable internet, leading to discrepancies in listed inventory.

Our Mitigation Strategy

We are piloting a "store-and-forward" feature where a Digital Champion with better connectivity can act as a local sync point for their community. We are also working with a local telecom provider to explore signal booster options for the community center.

💵

Building Trust in Digital Transactions

+

The shift from cash-on-delivery to app-based pricing and payments remains a key trust hurdle. Farmers are hesitant to commit to prices that may fluctuate and prefer the immediacy of cash.

Our Mitigation Strategy

We are structuring the first pilot transactions with a "hybrid" model. The app will determine the price based on quality and market rates, but payment will be made in cash immediately upon delivery for these initial runs. This allows farmers to experience the fair pricing mechanism without the barrier of digital payment uncertainty.
