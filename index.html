Challenges & Mitigations

Navigating the Hard Problems

Every rollout surfaces real barriers. Here is how we are meeting each one with practical, community-grounded solutions.

📚

Digital Literacy Barrier

+

A significant portion of farmers, particularly those over 50, continue to struggle with navigating the app independently, hindering use of dynamic pricing and inventory listing.

Our Mitigation Strategy

Digital Champions are running smaller weekly neighborhood clinics. We have also created a simplified picture-based user guide in the local dialect to complement one-on-one support.

📶

Intermittent Connectivity Issues

+

Synchronization errors can occur in areas with extremely unstable internet, leading to discrepancies in listed inventory even with offline mode enabled.

Our Mitigation Strategy

We are piloting a store-and-forward feature where a Digital Champion with better connectivity acts as a local sync point. We are also working with a local telecom provider on signal boosters for the community center.

💵

Building Trust in Digital Transactions

+

The shift from cash-on-delivery to app-based pricing remains a key trust hurdle. Farmers are hesitant about fluctuating prices and prefer the immediacy of cash.

Our Mitigation Strategy

The first pilot transactions use a hybrid model — the app determines the price based on quality and market rates, but payment is made in cash immediately upon delivery.
